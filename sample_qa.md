**Q1: What is the expense ratio of the Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund?**
A: The Direct Plan has an expense ratio of 0.54%, while the Regular Plan has an expense ratio of 1.54%. Direct plans are lower cost because they have no distributor commission.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-large-cap-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025

---

**Q2: What is the ELSS lock-in period for Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund?**
A: The Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund has a mandatory 3-year lock-in period. Each SIP instalment has its own 3-year lock-in starting from that instalment's date of investment. Early redemption is not permitted.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-elss-tax-saver-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025

---

**Q3: What is the minimum SIP amount for the Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund?**
A: The minimum SIP investment for the Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund is ₹500 per instalment (in multiples of ₹500 thereafter). The minimum lumpsum investment is also ₹500.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-elss-tax-saver-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025

---

**Q4: What is the exit load on the Mirae Asset Flexi Cap Fund?**
A: The exit load is 1% on units exceeding 15% of the investment if redeemed within 1 year from the date of investment. There is no exit load after 1 year.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-flexi-cap-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025

---

**Q5: What is the benchmark of the Mirae Asset Large & Midcap Fund?**
A: The benchmark for the Mirae Asset Large & Midcap Fund is the NIFTY Large Midcap 250 Total Return Index (TRI). This index tracks the top 250 companies by market capitalisation across both large cap and mid cap segments.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-large-midcap-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025

---

**Q6: What is the riskometer rating of the Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund?**
A: The Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund is rated 'Very High' on the SEBI-mandated riskometer. This means investors should understand that their principal investment in this fund will be at very high risk.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-large-cap-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025

**Q7: How do I download my capital gains statement from Groww?**
A: On the Groww app: Tap 'You' (bottom-right) → 'SIP & Reports' → 'Capital Gain' → Select financial year → Tap 'Download'. On the Groww website: Login → Profile → Reports → Tax Filing → 'Capital Gains - Mutual Funds' → Select FY → Download (Excel format).
Source: https://groww.in/help/mutual-funds/mf-others/how-to-download-capital-gain-report--50
Last updated: April 2025

**Q8: What is the minimum SIP for the Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund?**
A: The minimum SIP investment for the Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund is ₹99 per instalment (in multiples of ₹1 thereafter). The minimum lumpsum investment is ₹5,000.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-large-cap-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025

**Q9: Does the Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund qualify for Section 80C tax deduction?**
A: Yes, the Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund qualifies for a tax deduction of up to ₹1,50,000 per financial year under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act. This is an ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme), which comes with a mandatory 3-year lock-in.
Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/mirae-asset-elss-tax-saver-fund-direct-growth
Last updated: April 2025
